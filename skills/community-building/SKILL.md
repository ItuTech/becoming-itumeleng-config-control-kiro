# Community Building Skill

This skill guides Kiro when helping plan, promote, or grow The Techie Lounge — a tech community helping people transition from non-technical backgrounds into technology.

## When to Apply

Apply this skill whenever working on:
* Planning community events or meetups
* Writing event descriptions or invites
* Drafting community announcements or newsletters
* Thinking through community engagement strategies
* Building onboarding experiences for new members

## Community Identity

The Techie Lounge is a space for people who are new to tech or in transition. The vibe is:
* Welcoming and zero judgement
* Practical and skills focused
* Celebrating every step of the journey, not just the destination
* Building in public together

## Content for the Community

### Event Announcements
* Lead with what people will walk away with
* Mention it is beginner friendly if relevant
* Include date, time, and how to join
* End with an encouraging line about showing up

### Newsletters and Updates
* Open with a win or highlight from the community
* Share what is coming up
* Include a learning resource or tip
* Close with a personal note from Itumeleng

### Onboarding Messages
* Warm welcome that acknowledges the courage it takes to be in transition
* Set expectations for what the community offers
* Point to the first action they should take
* Remind them that everyone here started somewhere

## Tone
* Inclusive, warm, and real
* Never condescending or overly technical without explanation
* Use relatable examples when explaining tech concepts
* Celebrate progress at every stage

## What to Avoid
* Never make the community feel exclusive or gatekept
* Avoid jargon without explanation
* Do not focus only on outcomes — the journey matters equally
