# Content Creation Skill

This skill guides Kiro when helping with content creation for the Becoming Itumeleng brand across YouTube, LinkedIn, and Medium.

## When to Apply

Apply this skill whenever working on:
* Writing or reviewing LinkedIn posts
* Drafting YouTube video descriptions, titles, or scripts
* Writing Medium articles or outlines
* Creating hooks, calls to action, or engagement questions

## Platform Rules

### LinkedIn
* Start every post with an emoji hook that grabs attention in the first line
* Use bold section headers with relevant emojis (🌟 🧠 🌍 ☁️)
* End with an engagement question like "What excites you most about this?"
* Add 3 to 5 relevant hashtags at the end
* Place article links at the bottom or suggest adding in comments
* Close with a personal, hopeful thought that connects back to the journey

### YouTube
* Titles should be clear, curiosity-driven, and searchable
* Descriptions should open with the value proposition in the first 2 lines
* Include timestamps for videos longer than 5 minutes
* End descriptions with links to community and relevant resources

### Medium
* Articles should open with a personal connection or story
* Use subheadings every 3 to 4 paragraphs to break up the read
* Write in first person, warm and honest
* End with a reflection or call to action that connects to the community

## Voice and Tone
* Warm, encouraging, and real
* Write with exclamation energy without being over the top
* Flowing natural sentences, no choppy mini sentences
* No dashes in written content
* No hyphenated compound adjectives

## What to Avoid
* Never mention job titles or specific work responsibilities
* Never make up achievements or outcomes
* No identifiable company buildings or branding
* No filming at home or morning routine content
